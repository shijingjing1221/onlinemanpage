NAME sleep - delay for a specified amount of time

SYNOPSIS sleep NUMBER[SUFFIX]... sleep OPTION

DESCRIPTION Pause for NUMBER seconds. SUFFIX may be s for seconds (the default), m for minutes, h for hours or d for days. Unlike most implemen- tations that require NUMBER be an integer, here NUMBER may be an arbi- trary floating point number.

--help display this help and exit

--version output version information and exit
