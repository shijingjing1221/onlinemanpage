NAME FcMatrixEqual - Compare two matrices

SYNOPSIS #include <fontconfig.h>

void FcMatrixEqual (const FcMatrix *matrix1, const FcMatrix *matrix2);

DESCRIPTION FcMatrixEqual compares matrix1 and matrix2 returning FcTrue when they are equal and FcFalse when they are not.
