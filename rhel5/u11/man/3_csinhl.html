NAME csinh, csinhf, csinhl - complex hyperbolic sine

SYNOPSIS #include <complex.h>

double complex csinh(double complex z); float complex csinhf(float complex z); long double complex csinhl(long double complex z);

Link with -lm.

DESCRIPTION The complex hyperbolic sine function sinh(z) is defined as (exp(z)-exp(-z))/2.

CONFORMING TO C99

SEE ALSO cabs(3), ccosh(3), ctanh(3), complex(7)
