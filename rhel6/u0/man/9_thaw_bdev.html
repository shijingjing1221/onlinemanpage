NAME thaw_bdev - - unlock filesystem

SYNOPSIS int thaw_bdev(struct block_device * bdev, struct super_block * sb);

ARGUMENTS bdev blockdevice to unlock

sb associated superblock

DESCRIPTION Unlocks the filesystem and marks it writeable again after freeze_bdev.
