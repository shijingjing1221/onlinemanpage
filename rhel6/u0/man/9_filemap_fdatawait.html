FILEMAP_FDATAWAIT(9) Memory Management in Linux FILEMAP_FDATAWAIT(9)

NAME filemap_fdatawait - wait for all under-writeback pages to complete

SYNOPSIS int filemap_fdatawait(struct address_space * mapping);

ARGUMENTS mapping address space structure to wait for

DESCRIPTION Walk the list of under-writeback pages of the given address space and wait for all of them.
