PCI_STOP_BUS_DEVICE(9) Hardware Interfaces PCI_STOP_BUS_DEVICE(9)

NAME pci_stop_bus_device - stop a PCI device and any children

SYNOPSIS void pci_stop_bus_device(struct pci_dev * dev);

ARGUMENTS dev the device to stop

DESCRIPTION Stop a PCI device (detach the driver, remove from the global list and so on). This also stop any subordinate buses and children in a depth-first manner.
