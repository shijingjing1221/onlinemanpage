SAS_RPHY_DELETE(9) SCSI mid layer SAS_RPHY_DELETE(9)

NAME sas_rphy_delete - remove and free SAS remote PHY

SYNOPSIS void sas_rphy_delete(struct sas_rphy * rphy);

ARGUMENTS rphy SAS remote PHY to remove and free

DESCRIPTION Removes the specified SAS remote PHY and frees it.
