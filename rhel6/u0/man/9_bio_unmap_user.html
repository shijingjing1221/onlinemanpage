NAME bio_unmap_user - unmap a bio

SYNOPSIS void bio_unmap_user(struct bio * bio);

ARGUMENTS bio the bio being unmapped

DESCRIPTION Unmap a bio previously mapped by bio_map_user. Must be called with a process context.

bio_unmap_user may sleep.
