NAME pci_remove_behind_bridge - remove all devices behind a PCI bridge

SYNOPSIS void pci_remove_behind_bridge(struct pci_dev * dev);

ARGUMENTS dev PCI bridge device

DESCRIPTION Remove all devices on the bus, except for the parent bridge. This also removes any child buses, and any devices they may contain in a depth-first manner.
