SND_DMA_FREE_PAGES(9) Management of Cards and Device SND_DMA_FREE_PAGES(9)

NAME snd_dma_free_pages - release the allocated buffer

SYNOPSIS void snd_dma_free_pages(struct snd_dma_buffer * dmab);

ARGUMENTS dmab the buffer allocation record to release

DESCRIPTION Releases the allocated buffer via snd_dma_alloc_pages.
