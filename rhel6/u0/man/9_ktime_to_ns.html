NAME ktime_to_ns - convert a ktime_t variable to scalar nanoseconds

SYNOPSIS s64 ktime_to_ns(const ktime_t kt);

ARGUMENTS kt the ktime_t variable to convert

DESCRIPTION Returns the scalar nanoseconds representation of kt
