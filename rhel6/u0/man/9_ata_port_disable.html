ATA_PORT_DISABLE(9) libata Library ATA_PORT_DISABLE(9)

NAME ata_port_disable - Disable port.

SYNOPSIS void ata_port_disable(struct ata_port * ap);

ARGUMENTS ap Port to be disabled.

DESCRIPTION Modify ap data structure such that the system thinks that the entire port is disabled, and should never attempt to probe or communicate with devices on this port.

LOCKING host lock, or some other form of serialization.
