NAME netif_stacked_transfer_operstate - transfer operstate

SYNOPSIS void netif_stacked_transfer_operstate(const struct net_device * rootdev, struct net_device * dev);

ARGUMENTS rootdev the root or lower level device to transfer state from

dev the device to transfer operstate to

DESCRIPTION Transfer operational state from root to device. This is normally called when a stacking relationship exists between the root device and the device(a leaf device).
