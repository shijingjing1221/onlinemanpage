PARPORT_CLOSE(9) Parallel Port Devices PARPORT_CLOSE(9)

NAME parport_close - close a device opened with parport_open

SYNOPSIS void parport_close(struct pardevice * dev);

ARGUMENTS dev device to close

DESCRIPTION This is to parport_open as parport_unregister_device is to parport_register_device.
