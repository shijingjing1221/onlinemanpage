NAME flush_kthread_worker - flush all current works on a kthread_worker

SYNOPSIS void flush_kthread_worker(struct kthread_worker * worker);

ARGUMENTS worker worker to flush

DESCRIPTION Wait until all currently executing or pending works on worker are finished.
