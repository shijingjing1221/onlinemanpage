NAME usb_hub_for_each_child - iterate over all child devices on the hub

SYNOPSIS usb_hub_for_each_child(hdev, port1, child);

ARGUMENTS hdev USB device belonging to the usb hub

port1 portnum associated with child device

child child device pointer
