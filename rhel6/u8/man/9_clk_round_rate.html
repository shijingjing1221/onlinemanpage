CLK_ROUND_RATE(9) Clock Framework CLK_ROUND_RATE(9)

NAME clk_round_rate - adjust a rate to the exact rate a clock can provide

SYNOPSIS long clk_round_rate(struct clk * clk, unsigned long rate);

ARGUMENTS clk clock source

rate desired clock rate in Hz

DESCRIPTION Returns rounded clock rate in Hz, or negative errno.
