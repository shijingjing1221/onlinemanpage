NAME sync_inode - write an inode and its pages to disk.

SYNOPSIS int sync_inode(struct inode * inode, struct writeback_control * wbc);

ARGUMENTS inode the inode to sync

wbc controls the writeback mode

DESCRIPTION sync_inode will write an inode and its pages to disk. It will also correctly update the inode on its superblock´s dirty inode lists and will update inode->i_state.

The caller must have a ref on the inode.
