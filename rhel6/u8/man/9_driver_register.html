DRIVER_REGISTER(9) Device drivers infrastructure DRIVER_REGISTER(9)

NAME driver_register - register driver with bus

SYNOPSIS int driver_register(struct device_driver * drv);

ARGUMENTS drv driver to register

DESCRIPTION We pass off most of the work to the bus_add_driver call, since most of the things we have to do deal with the bus structures.
