NAME casinh, casinhf, casinhl - complex arc hyperbolic sine functions

SYNOPSIS #include <complex.h>

double complex casinh(double complex z); float complex casinhf(float complex z); long double complex casinhl(long double complex z);

DESCRIPTION These functions shall compute the complex arc hyperbolic sine of z, with branch cuts outside the interval [-i, +i] along the imaginary axis.

RETURN VALUE These functions shall return the complex arc hyperbolic sine value, in the range of a strip mathematically unbounded along the real axis and in the interval [-ipi/2, +ipi/2] along the imaginary axis.

ERRORS No errors are defined.
