SKB_MORPH(9) Linux Networking SKB_MORPH(9)

NAME skb_morph - morph one skb into another

SYNOPSIS struct sk_buff * skb_morph(struct sk_buff * dst, struct sk_buff * src);

ARGUMENTS dst the skb to receive the contents

src the skb to supply the contents

DESCRIPTION This is identical to skb_clone except that the target skb is supplied by the user.

The target skb is returned upon exit.
