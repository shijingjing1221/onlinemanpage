CLEAR_INODE(9) The Linux VFS CLEAR_INODE(9)

NAME clear_inode - clear an inode

SYNOPSIS void clear_inode(struct inode * inode);

ARGUMENTS inode inode to clear

DESCRIPTION This is called by the filesystem to tell us that the inode is no longer useful. We just terminate it with extreme prejudice.
