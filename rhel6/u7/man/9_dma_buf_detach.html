NAME dma_buf_detach - Remove the given attachment from dmabuf´s attachments list; optionally calls detach of dma_buf_ops for device-specific detach

SYNOPSIS void dma_buf_detach(struct dma_buf * dmabuf, struct dma_buf_attachment * attach);

ARGUMENTS dmabuf [in] buffer to detach from.

attach [in] attachment to be detached; is free´d after this call.
