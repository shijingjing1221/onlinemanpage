SND_POWER_WAIT(9) Management of Cards and Device SND_POWER_WAIT(9)

NAME snd_power_wait - wait until the power-state is changed.

SYNOPSIS int snd_power_wait(struct snd_card * card, unsigned int power_state);

ARGUMENTS card soundcard structure

power_state expected power state

DESCRIPTION Waits until the power-state is changed.

NOTE the power lock must be active before call.
