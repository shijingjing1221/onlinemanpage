NAME struct_ff_rumble_effect - defines parameters of a periodic force-feedback effect

SYNOPSIS struct ff_rumble_effect { __u16 strong_magnitude; __u16 weak_magnitude; };

MEMBERS strong_magnitude magnitude of the heavy motor

weak_magnitude magnitude of the light one

DESCRIPTION Some rumble pads have two motors of different weight. Strong_magnitude represents the magnitude of the vibration generated by the heavy one.
