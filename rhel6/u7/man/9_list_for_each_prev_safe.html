NAME list_for_each_prev_safe - iterate over a list backwards safe against removal of list entry

SYNOPSIS list_for_each_prev_safe(pos, n, head);

ARGUMENTS pos the struct list_head to use as a loop cursor.

n another struct list_head to use as temporary storage

head the head for your list.
