NAME pci_destroy_slot - decrement refcount for physical PCI slot

SYNOPSIS void pci_destroy_slot(struct pci_slot * slot);

ARGUMENTS slot struct pci_slot to decrement

DESCRIPTION struct pci_slot is refcounted, so destroying them is really easy; we just call kobject_put on its kobj and let our release methods do the rest.
