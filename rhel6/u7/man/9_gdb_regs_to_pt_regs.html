NAME gdb_regs_to_pt_regs - Convert GDB regs to ptrace regs.

SYNOPSIS void gdb_regs_to_pt_regs(unsigned long * gdb_regs, struct pt_regs * regs);

ARGUMENTS gdb_regs A pointer to hold the registers we´ve received from GDB.

regs A pointer to a struct pt_regs to hold these values in.

DESCRIPTION Convert the GDB regs in gdb_regs into the pt_regs, and store them in regs.
