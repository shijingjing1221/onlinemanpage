NAME struct_ff_ramp_effect - defines parameters of a ramp force-feedback effect

SYNOPSIS struct ff_ramp_effect { __s16 start_level; __s16 end_level; struct ff_envelope envelope; };

MEMBERS start_level beginning strength of the effect; may be negative

end_level final strength of the effect; may be negative

envelope envelope data
