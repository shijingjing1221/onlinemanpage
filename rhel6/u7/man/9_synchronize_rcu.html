NAME synchronize_rcu - wait until a grace period has elapsed.

SYNOPSIS void synchronize_rcu(void);

ARGUMENTS void no arguments

DESCRIPTION Control will return to the caller some time after a full grace period has elapsed, in other words after all currently executing RCU read-side critical sections have completed. RCU read-side critical sections are delimited by rcu_read_lock and rcu_read_unlock, and may be nested.
