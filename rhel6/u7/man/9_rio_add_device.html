NAME rio_add_device - Adds a RIO device to the device model

SYNOPSIS int rio_add_device(struct rio_dev * rdev);

ARGUMENTS rdev RIO device

DESCRIPTION Adds the RIO device to the global device list and adds the RIO device to the RIO device list. Creates the generic sysfs nodes for an RIO device.
