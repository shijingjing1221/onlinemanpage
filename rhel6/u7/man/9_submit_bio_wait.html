NAME submit_bio_wait - submit a bio, and wait until it completes

SYNOPSIS int submit_bio_wait(int rw, struct bio * bio);

ARGUMENTS rw whether to READ or WRITE, or maybe to READA (read ahead)

bio The struct bio which describes the I/O

DESCRIPTION Simple wrapper around submit_bio. Returns 0 on success, or the error from bio_endio on failure.
