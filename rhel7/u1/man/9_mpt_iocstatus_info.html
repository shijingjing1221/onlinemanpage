NAME

mpt_iocstatus_info − IOCSTATUS information returned from IOC.

ARGUMENTS

ioc

Pointer to MPT_ADAPTER structure

ioc_status

U32 IOCStatus word from IOC

mf

Pointer to MPT request frame

Refer to lsi/mpi.h.
