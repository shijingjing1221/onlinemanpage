NAME

composite − overlaps one image over another.

SYNOPSIS

composite [ options ... ] change-file base-file [ mask-file ]
output-image

OVERVIEW

The composite program is a member of the ImageMagick(1) suite of tools. Use it to overlap one image over another.

For more information about the composite command, point your browser to file:///usr/share/doc/ImageMagick-6.7.8/www/composite.html or http://www.imagemagick.org/script/composite.php.
