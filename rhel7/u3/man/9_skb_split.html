NAME

skb_split − Split fragmented skb to two parts at length len.

SYNOPSIS

void skb_split(struct sk_buff * skb, struct sk_buff * skb1, const u32 len);

ARGUMENTS

skb

the buffer to split

skb1

the buffer to receive the second part

len

new length for skb
