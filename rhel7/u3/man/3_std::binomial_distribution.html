template<typename _IntType = int>class std::binomial_distribution< _IntType >
A discrete binomial random number distribution.

The formula for the binomial probability density function is $p(i|t,p) n o m = {} p^i (1 - p)^{t - i}$ where $t$ and $p$ are the parameters of the t } { distribution.
