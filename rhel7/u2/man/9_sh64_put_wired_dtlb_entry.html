NAME

sh64_put_wired_dtlb_entry − Free a wired (locked−in) entry in the DTLB.

SYNOPSIS

int sh64_put_wired_dtlb_entry(unsigned long long entry);

ARGUMENTS

entry

Address of TLB slot.

DESCRIPTION

Works like a stack, last one to allocate must be first one to free.
