NAME

mpt_add_chain − Place a 32 bit chain SGE at address pAddr.

SYNOPSIS

void mpt_add_chain(void * pAddr, u8 next, u16 length, dma_addr_t dma_addr);

ARGUMENTS

pAddr

virtual address for SGE

next

nextChainOffset value (u32's)

length

length of next SGL segment

dma_addr

Physical address
